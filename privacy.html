Privacy Policy

Data storage

Feelora stores all your data only on your device. Your mood entries, journal entries, and settings are never sent to our servers or any third party. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to your personal data.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification (name, email, etc.)
  • Mood or journal content
  • Usage analytics or diagnostics
  • Location or device identifiers

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, Feelora uses local notifications on your device. Notification content (e.g. motivational quotes) is generated on your device. No notification data is sent to us.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at this page. Continued use of Feelora after changes means you accept the updated policy.
